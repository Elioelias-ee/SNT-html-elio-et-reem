Healthy Meals & Sleep

Simple quick and healthy meal ideas for one day :

Meal Calories (approx) Ingredients Preparation steps
Breakfast: Greek yogurt bowl ~350 kcal Greek yogurt, honey, berries, oats
  1. Place yogurt in a bowl.
  2. Add berries and a spoon of oats.
  3. Drizzle a little honey and serve.
Lunch: Grilled chicken salad ~450 kcal Chicken breast, mixed greens, olive oil, lemon
  1. Grill chicken and slice.
  2. Toss greens with olive oil and lemon.
  3. Add chicken on top and serve.
Snack: Apple & peanut butter ~200 kcal Apple, 1 tbsp peanut butter Slice apple and enjoy with peanut butter.
Dinner: Veggie stir-fry with rice ~500 kcal Mixed vegetables, soy sauce, rice
  1. Stir-fry vegetables with a splash of soy sauce.
  2. Serve with cooked rice.

The importance of sleep

Good sleep helps recovery, mental clarity, and energy for workouts.

Age Recommended hours
Children (6–12) 9–12 hours
Teens (13–17) 8–10 hours
Adults (18–64) 7–9 hours
Older adults (65+) 7–8 hours

Short tips for better sleep:

  • Keep a regular bedtime.
  • Turn off screens before bed.
  • Keep the room dark and cool.
