Workouts: Home & Gym

Below is a table of easy exercises. Each row shows where you can do it, recommended time, and an approximate calories burned.

Exercise Place Recommended time Approx calories burned
Jumping jacks Home 10 minutes ~80 kcal
Bodyweight squats Home 3 sets × 12 (≈15 minutes) ~70 kcal
Plank Home 3 × 45 seconds ~30 kcal
Treadmill run Gym 20 minutes ~250 kcal
Stationary bike Gym 30 minutes ~280 kcal
Weight circuit (moderate) Gym 30 minutes ~220 kcal

Tips:

  1. Start each session with a 5-minute warm-up.
  2. Cool down and stretch after exercising.
  3. Adjust time and intensity to your level.
